About The School

Kandiege Primary and Junior Secondary School is a public institution serving over 600 learners with more than 40 qualified teachers and 10 support staff. The school is committed to competency-based education, academic excellence, discipline, leadership, and innovation.

Mission

To provide quality education that empowers learners academically, socially, and morally.

Vision

To be a center of excellence in nurturing responsible and innovative global citizens.

Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Discipline
  • Innovation
  • Respect
  • Teamwork
